Information Engineering

About the Branch

The department conducts scientific researches in the field of Information technology and its different related applications and researches are being conducted in the fields of computer networks, neural networks, intelligent networks, virtual reality, internet programming and the information engineering with its protection and security.

Vision

Information Engineering branch aims to be one of the outstanding leading institutions in our country. It is distinguished amongst other scientific fields in interesting of modern effective and cooperative information systems. Information Engineering branch can efficiently satisfy the marketing and scientific requirements towards research and practical life.

Mission

The mission of Information Engineering branch focuses on preparing Information Engineers and scientific researchers with high standards of knowledge and intellectual ability. This is to complete the scientific mission of our country with the other fields of engineering. The Information Engineering branch considers the quality assurance for the engineering program and full commitments of the professional ethics.

Objectives

• Providing well qualified graduates specialized in Information Engineering to serve and participate in achieving the needs and goals of our country development and maintain academic staff for higher education studying in Computer Engineering Department.

• Focusing on scientific research of Information Engineering field to be a major part of developing and enhancing the provided scientific studies and consultancy work to the governmental sectors of mutual interest.

• The establishment of new trends in education and training to cope with the recent developments in science and technology in accordance with society needs.

• Pursuit towards the rapid development in information systems and technology through the attainment of many projects inside and outside the university.

• Continuous evaluation and enhancement of all levels of Information Engineering program including the undergraduate and postgraduate studies.

• Continuous evaluation and enhancement of the employed curriculums and find out the problems to be solved and benefits to be adopted.
